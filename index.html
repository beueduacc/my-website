What is a Raspberry Pi?

Raspberry Pi is a popular line of open design, modular, single-board computers. While they aren't the most powerful computers in terms of performance, they have become popular with hobbyists as well as professionals in tech for their affordability, expandability, and wide variety of use cases.

Some popular use cases for Raspberry Pi include...

  • Small form factor workstations
  • Video game emulators
  • Media server hosts
  • Smart home controllers
  • and so much more
