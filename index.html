Welcome to Raspberry Pi: The Everything Computer!
Discover the power of Raspberry Pi, a tiny yet mighty computer built on a foundation of open source hardware and software. Whether you’re a tinkerer, a coder, part of a large industry, or brand new to computing; these affordable and accessible computing solutions empower you to create, learn, and innovate.

Learn, Create, and Make with Raspberry Pi!
The Raspberry Pi is more than just hardware; it’s a gateway to endless possibilities. Dive into programming, build practical projects, and explore the world of computing. From classrooms to kitchen tables, Raspberry Pi makes learning fun and accessible. Join our community of makers, educators, and enthusiasts as we push the boundaries of what’s possible. Whether you’re playing retro games, building your own media servers, or simply experimenting with code, Raspberry Pi is a great platform to start experimenting with computers.
